# Oral history interview of Urgyen La  
Urgyen [Tib. ཨོ་རྒྱན] was a well-known Drepung Loseling monk who at one time held the powerful position of Chagdampa. In this interview, he discusses his experiences doing trading in India in Kalimpong and Kolkata.
