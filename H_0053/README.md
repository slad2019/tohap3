# Oral history interview of Li Zuomin  
Li Zuomin was the director [Ch. qu zhang] of the United Front Work Bureau [Tib. འཐབ་ཕྱོགས་གཅིག་གྱུར་བུ] in the Tibet Work Committee [Tib. བོད་ལས་དོན, Ch. wei yuan Tib. ལྷན་ཁང].
