# Oral history interview of Geshe Thubden Thuwang  
Thubden Thuwang [Tib. ཐུབ་བསྟན་མཐུ་དབང] was a well-known Geshe from Sera Jey College. In this interview, he talks in detail about the aftermath of the Lhundrup Dzong incident and the ensuing confrontation between the government and the Sera Jey College.
