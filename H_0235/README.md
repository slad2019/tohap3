# Oral history interview of Surkhang Wangchen Gelek


Surkhang, Wangchen Gelek [Tib. zur khang, dbang chen dge legs] was one of the most important Tibetan government officials in modern Tibetan history. He served as a Kashag or Cabinet Minister (KaloÌˆn) from 1943-1959. In this interview, he discusses the period of the death of the 13th Dalai Lama.
