# Oral history interview of Setrong Wangye  
Setrong Wangye was a member of a rich shungyupa (government taxpayer family [tib. gshung rgyugs pa]) in TuÌˆlung [tib. stod lung] district. His family had large land holdings with its own miser [tib. mi ser] or bound peasants/serfs and also held the position of Gyatso gembo [Tib. rgya mtsho rgan po] for the district.
