# Oral history interview of Tara Tenzin Chonyi


Tara, Tenzin ChoÌˆnyi [Tib. rta ra, bstan 'dzin chos nyid] was a monk official who worked in the Dalai Lama's Bureau Office in Beijing from 1952-5. Later, he headed the Nangmagang Office in the Dalai Lama's Secretariat. In this interview, he discusses being sent on an inspection tour of China and his experiences there.
